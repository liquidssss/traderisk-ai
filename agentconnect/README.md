# AgentConnect MVP Plan (Refined)

## **The Vision**
A professional B2B connection and management platform for **Replica Agents** (CNfans, KakoBuy, etc.) and **Content Creators**. 

**Core Focus:** Connection, Discovery, and Relationship Management (CRM) for the rep world.

---

## **Refined MVP Features**

### **1. Connection & Discovery (The Bridge)**
- **Creator Directory:** Agents search/filter creators by platform (TikTok/YT/IG), niche, follower count, and engagement.
- **Agent Directory:** Creators can find verified agents looking for promotion.
- **Application Flow:** Creators apply to "Opportunities" posted by agents. 
- **Direct Messaging:** Secure communication to discuss private contract terms.

### **2. Relationship Management (The CRM)**
- **Campaign Tracker:** Manage the status of multiple creators (Pending, In-Contract, Content Posted, Completed).
- **Private Contracts:** Payouts and contract terms are handled externally (Agents prefer privacy). AgentConnect tracks the *status* only.
- **Performance Logging:** Creators log their results (views/clicks) for agents to review.

### **3. Account & Personalization**
- **In-depth Sidebar:** Navigation for Marketplace, Campaigns, My Profile, Settings.
- **Account Settings:** Manage profile stats, platform links, and notification preferences.
- **Verification:** Creators verify their social accounts to prove their reach.

---

## **Strategic Decisions (Updated)**

1. **Privacy First:** Agents' specific contract terms and payouts are **NOT** handled on-platform to respect the industry's need for confidentiality.
2. **Discovery Engine:** Focus on helping **new creators** get noticed by providing a "Discovery" feed for agents.
3. **Status over Payouts:** We track "Work Done" and "Milestones reached" rather than handling the money.
