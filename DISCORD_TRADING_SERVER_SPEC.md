# Discord Trading Server Spec
**Futures Trading Community | Beginner to Intermediate Focus**

---

## 1. SERVER STRUCTURE & CATEGORIES

### Category 1: WELCOME
- `#welcome` — Server intro, rules, getting started
- `#announcements` — Major updates, new features, events
- `#faq` — Common questions answered

### Category 2: COMMUNITY
- `#introductions` — New members introduce themselves
- `#general` — Off-topic, casual chat, memes
- `#wins-testimonials` — Trade wins, success stories, screenshots
- `#crypto-chat` — Crypto discussion (separate from futures)

### Category 3: TRADING
- `#daily-market-outlook` — Morning/evening market analysis (pinned daily)
- `#trade-ideas` — Setup ideas, entry/exit points, thesis
- `#live-trading-chat` — Real-time trading during market hours
- `#trade-recaps` — End-of-day/week reviews, lessons learned
- `#beginner-questions` — No judgment zone for new traders

### Category 4: EDUCATION
- `#resources` — PDFs, video links, strategy guides, checklists
- `#strategy-breakdown` — Deep dives into specific setups
- `#risk-management` — Position sizing, stop losses, psychology

### Category 5: PREMIUM (Private)
- `#premium-setups` — Exclusive trade ideas (premium only)
- `#live-sessions` — Recordings & notes from live trading calls
- `#premium-chat` — Private community for paid members
- `#1on1-booking` — Link to calendar for coaching calls

### Category 6: SUPPORT
- `#bugs-feedback` — Report issues, suggest features
- `#mod-mail` — DM bot for private support requests

## 2. ROLES & PERMISSIONS MATRIX

| Role | Access | Permissions | Notes |
|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| **Admin** | All channels | Full control, manage roles, delete messages, ban | You + trusted co-mods |
| **Moderator** | All channels | Manage messages, mute/kick, pin messages | 2-3 active mods |
| **Premium Member** | All + private premium channels | Post in all channels, access exclusive content | $25/month |
| **Free Member** | Public channels only | Post in community/trading/education, read-only in premium | Default role |
| **VIP** | All + VIP lounge (optional) | Priority support, featured in wins, early access | Top traders or $50/month |
| **Bot** | Specific channels | Automated tasks (roles, moderation, payments) | MEE6, Dyno, etc. |

### Permission Details

**Free Members can:**
- Read all public channels
- Post in: #general, #introductions, #trade-ideas, #beginner-questions, #trade-recaps, #resources
- React to messages
- Cannot: Access premium channels, post in #daily-market-outlook (mod-only)

**Premium Members can:**
- Everything free members can do
- Access: #premium-setups, #live-sessions, #premium-chat, #1on1-booking
- Post in: #daily-market-outlook (optional, if you want premium input)
- Priority responses from mods

**Moderators can:**
- Manage all channels
- Pin/unpin messages
- Mute/kick/ban members
- Manage roles

## 3. MONETIZATION STRUCTURE

### Free Tier
**What they get:**
- Access to #welcome, #general, #introductions, #trade-ideas, #beginner-questions
- Read-only access to #daily-market-outlook, #resources, #strategy-breakdown
- Community engagement (wins, testimonials)
- Limited value → friction point for upgrade

### Premium Tier ($25/month)
**What they get:**
- All free tier access
- **#premium-setups** — 3-5 exclusive trade ideas per week (your best setups)
- **#live-sessions** — 2x weekly live trading calls (1 hour each, recorded)
  - Monday 9 AM ET (market open)
  - Thursday 2 PM ET (afternoon session)
- **#premium-chat** — Private community (faster responses, deeper discussions)
- **#1on1-booking** — 1 monthly 30-min coaching call (included)
- Early access to new strategies/resources
- Monthly performance recap (your trades + community stats)

### VIP Tier ($50/month) — Optional
**What they get:**
- Everything premium
- **2x monthly 1-on-1 calls** (instead of 1)
- **VIP lounge** — Private channel with top traders
- Featured in #wins-testimonials (highlighted)
- Direct DM access for urgent questions
- Custom strategy review (quarterly)

### Upgrade Funnel
1. **Free member joins** → Gets value immediately (market outlook, trade ideas)
2. **After 1 week** → Bot sends DM: "Enjoying the community? Premium members get exclusive setups + live trading calls"
3. **After 2 weeks** → Sees premium members posting wins in #wins-testimonials
4. **After 1 month** → Sees exclusive #premium-setups channel (teaser, can't access)
5. **Conversion point** → Premium member posts a win → Free member clicks upgrade link

Use MEE6 or Dyno bot to track:

- **Week 1:** New strategy workshop (live call)

- **Week 3:** Q&A with you (premium members get priority)

## 7. BRANDING & TONE

### Visual Identity
- **Color scheme:** Dark mode (Discord default) + accent color (e.g., electric blue, neon green)
- **Server icon:** Clean, professional trading-related logo (chart, candle, bull/bear)
- **Banner:** Sleek, minimal design with tagline

### Tone Guidelines
- **Confident but not arrogant** — "We're winning, but we're learning"
- **Sharp and direct** — No fluff, no "guru" vibes
- **Results-driven** — Focus on wins, lessons, growth
- **Supportive** — Help each other, celebrate wins, learn from losses
- **Professional** — This is a business, not a casino

### Example Messaging
❌ "Get rich quick with our secret strategy!"
✅ "Learn how to trade futures with consistent risk management"

❌ "I'm the best trader, follow my signals"
✅ "Here's my analysis. What do you think?"

❌ "MOON INCOMING 🚀🚀🚀"
✅ "Key resistance at 4200. If we break, next target is 4350"

## 8. BOT RECOMMENDATIONS & AUTOMATIONS

### Essential Bots

**1. MEE6 (Moderation + Leveling)**
- Auto-assign roles on join
- Leveling system (XP for messages)
- Moderation (mute, kick, ban)
- Custom commands
- Cost: Free tier sufficient

**2. Dyno (Moderation + Automation)**
- Auto-moderation (spam, links, caps)
- Custom commands
- Logging (deleted messages, bans)
- Cost: Free tier sufficient

**3. Stripe/Gumroad Bot (Payments)**
- Handle premium subscriptions
- Auto-assign premium role on payment
- Manage cancellations
- Cost: Depends on payment processor

**4. Reminder Bot (Scheduled Messages)**
- Daily market outlook reminder
- Live session reminders
- Weekly recap reminders
- Cost: Free

**5. Reaction Roles (Optional)**
- Members click emoji to get roles
- Simplifies onboarding
- Cost: Free

### Automation Workflows

**On Join:**
1. Send welcome DM
2. Assign "Free Member" role
3. Post intro prompt in #introductions

**Daily (9 AM ET):**
1. Post market outlook template in #daily-market-outlook
2. Send reminder to premium members: "Live session in 1 hour"

**Weekly (Friday 4 PM ET):**
1. Post weekly recap template
2. Announce leaderboard winners
3. Remind free members about premium upgrade

**Monthly:**
1. Post performance recap
2. Send renewal reminders to premium members
3. Announce new strategy workshop
